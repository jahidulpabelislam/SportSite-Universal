World Athletics Championships

The World Athletics Championships is a athletics competition with competitors from around the world. It is held every two years but originally was every four years. It has a variety of different events in the competition. The event is set up by the International Association of Athletics Federations (IAAF). It has been up and running since 1983.

Different types of events in the Athletics:

  • 100m
  • 200m
  • 400m
  • 800m
  • 5,000
  • 10,000
  • 110m hurdles
  • 400m hurdles
